Explore Ladakh: The Jewel of the Himalayas

Are you ready to embark on a journey to one of the most mesmerizing destinations on the planet? Our "Explore Ladakh" tour invites you to unravel the enchanting beauty of the Himalayas at an unbeatable cost.

Tour Name: Explore Ladakh - The Jewel of the Himalayas
Tour Cost: Starting at 80,000 rupees per person
Tour Dates: This exceptional adventure is available every year from May to September, allowing you to choose the perfect time to witness Ladakh's distinct charm.

Tour Highlights:

  • Breathtaking Scenery: Traverse through Ladakh's high passes, pristine lakes, and rugged landscapes. Marvel at the surreal beauty that has earned Ladakh the title of "Little Tibet."
  • Cultural Immersion: Immerse yourself in the rich Ladakhi culture. Engage with the warm and welcoming locals and gain insights into their unique traditions and way of life.
  • Thrilling Adventures: Embark on adrenaline-pumping activities such as trekking, river rafting, and jeep safaris, designed for adventure seekers.
  • Ladakhi Cuisine: Savor the delectable flavors of Ladakh with traditional meals, showcasing the region's culinary delights.

Tour Itinerary:

  • Day 1 - Arrival in Leh: Begin your journey in the vibrant town of Leh, where you'll acclimatize to the high altitude.
  • Day 2-3 - Explore Leh: Visit local monasteries and immerse yourself in Ladakh's rich culture.
  • Day 4-6 - Nubra Valley: Traverse the world-famous Khardung La pass and explore the scenic Nubra Valley.
  • Day 7-8 - Pangong Lake: Witness the changing colors of Pangong Lake, made famous by Bollywood movies.
  • Day 9-11 - Adventure in Hemis: Go on exhilarating treks and raft down the Zanskar River.
  • Day 12 - Departure: Bid farewell to Ladakh with memories that will last a lifetime.

Tour Inclusions:

  • Accommodation in comfortable hotels and camps.
  • All meals during the tour.
  • Experienced local guides.
  • Airport transfers.
  • All required permits.
